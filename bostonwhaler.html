The Background of Boston Whaler

Boston Whalers are a very respected boat that play a role in our society today. The reason that this boat is so great and well known is because they are considered unsinkable. These are labeled unsinkable due to their foam-filled hull design. These types of boat were founded by Richard Fischer in 1958, who was searching to create a fundamentally safer boat.

Different types of Boston Whalers

Boston Whaler has a big range of different boats that they produce to start off they have the 13 Super sport which is the smallest boat they have then the biggest in their fleet is a 42 foot outrage. Then they have different types of whaler that range in different types of hulls for the boats. The first being a Montauk, Dauntless, Outrage, Vantage, Conquest, and Realm. All these different boats have specific meaning and advantages. The montauk is the shallow boat where the hull is more round on the bottom and the largest they go is 21 feet. The Dauntless is similar but has a deeper V shape for the hull and it works better in choppier water. The Outrage is typically used for an offshore boat, very popular for fishermen. The vantage is a day boat, it is a bowrider and comes in a range of sizes such as 24 up to 33. The conquest is unique because it has a cabin in the front of the boat. Finally, the Realm is the bigger of the vantage with a similar layout but goes from 35 feet to 40 feet in sizes but also has a lot of cover from the wind due to the windshield that covers the entire cabin.

What to do on a Boston Whaler

A lot of people find joy in their Boston Whalers there tend to be a lot of things you can do. For example the owner can have day parties and go out for the day with their family and friends. Having a boat gives you access to a lot more places to explore, you can explore reefs along with going offshore and fishing for tuna.
